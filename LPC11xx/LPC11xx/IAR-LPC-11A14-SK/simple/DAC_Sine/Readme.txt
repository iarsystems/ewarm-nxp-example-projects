Purpose:
To produce a Sine wave using Systick timer on the Digital To Anlog (DAC) peripheral of LPC11A14

Running mode:
* Compile, Flash the program and reset.

Pins used:
P0_4 for AOUT 

How to use it?
>> The DAC outputs a Sine wave on pin P0_4. Can also be heard on the buzzer.


Tip:
*To vary the frequency of the sine wave obtained, change the Systick timer
